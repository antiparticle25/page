Welcome to my webpage!

I am fundamentally interested in the journey from genomics to personalized medicine, and in the actionable insights that data can provide in different societal fields. This includes applications not only in the development of biomedical solutions, but also market and business development.

Previously, I was a researcher at the Gulbenkian Institute of Science, the Berlin Institute for Medical Systems Biology/Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine, and ElementZero Biolabs. I also take pride in being a global citizen, having lived in Lisbon, Paris, Berlin, Rome, and Warsaw.

For a more detailed look at my background and experience, please feel free to view my resume or connect on LinkedIn.
