I am fundamentally interested in the journey from genomics to personalized medicine, and the actionable insights data can provide across various fields. This includes not only development of biomedical applications, but also market and business development strategies.

Previously, I was a researcher at the Gulbenkian Institute for Science, the Berlin Institute for Medical Systems Biology/Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine, and ElementZero Biolabs. I also take pride in being a global citizen, having resided in Lisbon, Paris, Berlin, Rome, and Warsaw.
